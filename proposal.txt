PROJECT PROPOSAL
Title
Heuristic Approach to Storage Resource Allocation for Cost Reduction and Service Level Agreement-
Aware Availability


1. Introduction
The rapid adoption of cloud computing has significantly increased the demand for efficient storage
resource management. Organizations rely on cloud storage services to provide reliable data availability
while maintaining operational costs within acceptable limits. However, improper allocation of storage
resources often leads to over-provisioning, under-utilization, increased operational expenses, and
potential violations of Service Level Agreements (SLAs).


Traditional storage allocation techniques may not adequately balance cost optimization and availability
requirements. Heuristic approaches offer practical solutions by generating near-optimal allocation
decisions within reasonable computational time. This project proposes the development of a heuristic-
based storage resource allocation system that minimizes storage costs while ensuring SLA-aware
availability requirements are met.


To demonstrate and evaluate the effectiveness of the proposed approach, a software platform will be
developed using Streamlit, providing visualization, monitoring, recommendation, and reporting
functionalities.


2. Problem Statement
Cloud service providers and organizations frequently face challenges in allocating storage resources
efficiently. Excessive resource allocation results in unnecessary costs, while insufficient allocation can
lead to SLA violations and reduced service availability.


There is a need for an intelligent allocation mechanism capable of balancing storage cost and
availability requirements while providing administrators with actionable recommendations and real-
time visibility into resource utilization.


3. Aim
To design and implement a heuristic-based storage resource allocation system that reduces storage
costs while maintaining SLA-aware availability requirements.


4. Objectives
     1. To study existing cloud storage allocation strategies and SLA management techniques.
     2. To design a heuristic algorithm for storage resource allocation.
     3. To develop a software platform for storage monitoring and optimization.
     4. To implement cost estimation and SLA compliance evaluation mechanisms.
     5. To generate storage allocation recommendations based on workload requirements.
